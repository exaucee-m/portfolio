About

Exaucee

Junior Front-End Web Developer

As a self-taught junior developer, my passion for coding and web development has driven me to continually seek out opportunities to improve my skills. While I may not have taken the traditional route towards becoming a developer, I have found that there are many resources available for those who are willing to put in the time and effort to learn.

The most valuable experiences I have had as a self-taught developer was completing bootcamp courses through SheCodes and Udemy as well as a virtual Technology internship with Bright Network. I was able to hone my skills and gain a deeper understanding of programming concepts through coding exercises, quizzes, tutored videos and capstone projects. These experiences helped me to not only understand the theoretical side of web development but also to code proficiently and design functional websites. My technical skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and more. I'm always pushing myself to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in web development.

As I continue to develop my skills as a junior developer, I am eager to take on new challenges and projects. I have a keen eye for design and I'm always thinking about ways to improve the user experience of the websites I create. I am a quick learner and am always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge, skillset and expertise in this field.
Overall, I am committed and excited about the future of my career as a self-taught junior developer!
